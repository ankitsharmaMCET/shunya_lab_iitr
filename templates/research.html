Our Research

Our research operates at the nexus of energy systems, supply chain management, and data-driven optimization to build a sustainable, net-zero future.

Our Focus Areas

Key domains where we build models, methods, and real-world insights.

Energy Systems & Grid Management

Developing optimization models for integrating renewable sources, ensuring grid stability, and managing energy storage.

Sustainable Supply Chains

Designing frameworks to decarbonize logistics, promote circular economy principles, and minimize environmental footprints.

Energy Policy & Economics

Analyzing policy frameworks, carbon markets, and economic incentives to accelerate sustainable energy adoption.

Energy Transition Modeling & Planning

Developing integrated assessment and optimization models to design low-carbon transition pathways, evaluate infrastructure investments, and support long-term energy planning.

Ongoing Projects

A snapshot of current work across policy, markets, and energy transitions.

Towards a Stable Indian Carbon Market: Design and Policy Insights

This project focuses on designing robust market mechanisms and policy frameworks to establish an efficient and stable carbon trading system in India.

Multi-Country International Electricity Transition Potential

We are modeling the techno-economic potential and policy requirements for cross-border electricity trade to enhance regional grid stability and renewable energy uptake.

Exploring Low Carbon Transportation Pathways for India

This research analyzes pathways for decarbonizing India's transport sector, focusing on EV adoption, green hydrogen, and the required supply chain infrastructure.
