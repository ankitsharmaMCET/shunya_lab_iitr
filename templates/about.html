About Shunya Lab

Pioneering data-driven solutions at the nexus of energy systems and sustainable supply chain management.

Who We Are

Shunya Lab is a premier research initiative within the Department of Management Studies at IIT Roorkee, dedicated to solving two of the world's most pressing challenges: energy transition and supply chain sustainability.

We operate at the critical intersection where energy optimization meets logistical efficiency. Our work involves applying the principles of strategic management—including advanced modeling, data analytics, and continuous optimization—to complex energy systems. This unique, interdisciplinary approach allows us to develop holistic solutions that are not only technologically sound but also economically viable and environmentally responsible.

Our Guiding Principles

Vision and mission that define how we work and why it matters.

Our Vision

To pioneer and develop cutting-edge workflows for the optimization of whole energy systems, setting a global standard for innovation and sustainability.

Our Mission

To minimize the environmental impact of supply chain operations for a sustainable future.
To maximize supply chain efficiency through strategic management and continuous optimization.

Our Approach

We bridge theory and practice by integrating rigorous analysis with real-world applications.

Data-Driven Modeling

We build sophisticated analytical and simulation models to forecast outcomes, identify bottlenecks, and test strategies before implementation.

Systems Thinking

We analyze energy and supply chains as interconnected systems, ensuring our solutions are holistic and account for complex dependencies.

Industry & Policy Integration

Our research is grounded in practical challenges, and we actively collaborate with industry partners and policymakers to drive tangible change.
